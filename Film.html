Maleficent

*Maleficent* is a 2014 American fantasy film directed by Robert Stromberg. The story serves as a retelling of *Sleeping Beauty* from the perspective of the villain, Maleficent, and explores the events that drove her to curse Princess Aurora.

Information

  • Release Date: May 30, 2014
  • Director: Robert Stromberg
  • Genre: Fantasy, Adventure
  • Running Time: 97 minutes

Cast

  • Angelina Jolie as Maleficent
  • Elle Fanning as Aurora
  • Sharlto Copley as King Stefan
  • Sam Riley as Diaval
  • Director: Robert Stromberg
  • Writer: Linda Woolverton

Characters

  • Maleficent: A powerful fairy betrayed by stefan.
  • Aurora: The cursed princess destined to sleep forever.
  • Diaval: Maleficent's loyal crow companion.
  • King Stefan: Aurora's father who betrays Maleficent.
