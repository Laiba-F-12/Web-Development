An Italian sauce that is usually combined with fettuccine noodles and is referred to as fettuccine Alfredo. The sauce consists of heavy cream or half and half that is mixed with butter, grated Parmesan cheese, pepper, and occasionally nutmeg to create a rich Italian meal.

Ingredientsüìã

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • 1/3 Tablespoon of Salt
  • 1/3 Tablespoon of Pepper
  • 1 Box (32 fl oz.) of Chicken Broth
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Heavy cream
  • 1/2 Cup of Parmesan Cheese

Method‚ú®

  1. Chop the Garlic and Chicken Breasts
  2. Put Oil and Garlic in the Pan
  3. Add Chicken to the Pan
  4. Add Butter, Salt and Pepper
  5. Add Chicken Broth
  6. Add Your Pasta
  7. Let It Soften
  8. Add Cream, Butter and Cheese
  9. Add Cheese
  10. And You Are Done!
